For Workers
No experience required. Browse jobs near your ZIP code, apply with one tap, and show up ready to work. No resume, no interview.
Once the hirer marks the job complete, funds are processed through Stripe and typically deposited to your bank within 2 business days for free. An instant payout option is also available for a 1% fee, with funds arriving in approximately 30 minutes to an eligible debit card.

GoDayWork charges a 7% platform fee on all job payments, paid by the hirer at checkout. There are no hidden fees. Tips are passed 100% to the worker with no platform fee.
