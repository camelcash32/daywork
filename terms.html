6. Payments

💳 All payments on GoDayWork are processed through Stripe. By using our payment features, you also agree to Stripe's Terms of Service. GoDayWork does not store your credit card information — all payment data is handled securely by Stripe.

Platform Fee

GoDayWork charges a 7% platform fee on all job payments. This fee is added on top of the job pay amount and is charged to the hirer at checkout. The platform fee is non-refundable once payment has been processed.

Example: If a job pays $100, the hirer will be charged $107 at checkout ($100 job payment + $7 platform fee). The worker receives payment for the agreed job amount.

Tips

Hirers may optionally add a tip for their worker at the time of payment. Tips are passed 100% to the worker — no platform fee is charged on tips. Tips are voluntary and not required. Once a tip is processed through Stripe, it is non-refundable.

Payment Processing

  • All payments are processed in US Dollars (USD)
  • Payment is collected from the hirer via Stripe Checkout at the time of payment
  • Workers receive payment as arranged — GoDayWork facilitates the transaction
  • A minimum payment of $0.50 is required by Stripe
  • Maximum payment per job is $200. Job listings may not exceed this amount.
  • GoDayWork is not responsible for payment disputes between hirers and workers beyond facilitating the transaction

Payment Types

When posting a job, hirers select one of the following payment structures:

  • Flat Rate — A fixed total amount paid to the worker upon job completion, regardless of time worked.
  • Per Hour — An hourly rate agreed upon at time of posting. The total payment collected at checkout is based on the stated hourly rate. GoDayWork does not track hours — it is the responsibility of the hirer and worker to agree on total hours worked.

Worker Payouts

Once a hirer marks a job as complete, the payment is released to the worker's connected Stripe account. Workers can choose how to receive their funds:

  • Standard Payout (Free) — Funds are deposited to your bank account within approximately 2 business days at no charge.
  • Instant Payout (1% fee, minimum $0.50) — Funds are sent to an eligible debit card within approximately 30 minutes. A 1% instant transfer fee (minimum $0.50) is deducted from your payout amount. Not all debit cards support instant payouts — eligibility is determined by Stripe.

Workers must connect a Stripe payout account via Settings to receive funds. GoDayWork is not responsible for delays caused by Stripe's processing times or bank processing times.

Refunds

Refund requests must be submitted within 48 hours of payment. To request a refund, use the refund request option in the app — you do not need to contact us by email. Refunds are reviewed by our team and, if approved, are processed automatically through Stripe directly back to the original payment method. You will not need to take any further action once a refund is approved.

  • The platform fee (7%) is non-refundable once payment has been processed
  • Tips are non-refundable once processed through Stripe
  • Refund decisions are made at the sole discretion of GoDayWork
  • Approved refunds typically appear on your statement within 5–10 business days depending on your bank

8. Ratings & Reviews

After a job is completed, both the hirer and the worker are required to rate each other. GoDayWork uses a blind rating system — neither party can see the other's rating until both have submitted. This prevents retaliation and ensures honest feedback.

  • A star rating (1–5) is required from both parties upon job completion
  • Ratings are submitted independently and remain hidden until both sides have rated
  • Once both parties submit, both ratings are revealed at the same time
  • If only one party submits, the rating stays hidden and does not count publicly
  • Written comments are optional — you may submit a star rating without a comment
  • You agree to leave honest and accurate ratings — false or manipulated ratings are prohibited
  • GoDayWork reserves the right to remove ratings that violate these terms
