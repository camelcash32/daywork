Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

DAYWORK ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website godaywork.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use DAYWORK:

  • Account information — username, email address, and password when you register
  • Profile information — bio, skills, location (ZIP code), and profile photo if provided
  • Job postings — job titles, descriptions, pay rates, locations, and photos you post
  • Messages — chat messages between job posters and workers
  • Ratings and reviews — feedback you leave for other users
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and device information

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the DAYWORK platform
  • To connect job posters with workers in their area
  • To send email notifications about jobs, applications, and account activity
  • To send verification codes when you create an account
  • To improve our service and fix bugs
  • To moderate content and enforce our Terms of Service

Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you personalized ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet. For more information, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these cases:

  • Other users — your username, profile, and job posts are visible to other DAYWORK users
  • Service providers — we use Resend for email delivery and Railway for hosting
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect our rights

Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Railway (railway.app). We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

Cookies

DAYWORK uses localStorage in your browser to keep you logged in and save your preferences. We also use cookies through Google AdSense for advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

Children's Privacy

DAYWORK is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of email notifications through your account settings
