Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

DAYWORK ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website godaywork.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use GoDayWork:

  • Account information — your real first and last name, email address, and password when you register. Usernames are not used; real legal names are required.
  • Profile information — phone number, bio, skills, location (ZIP code), gender, age, and profile photo submitted for verification
  • Verification data — your name approval status and photo approval status, reviewed manually by our administrators
  • Job postings — job titles, descriptions, pay rates, locations, and details you post
  • Messages — chat messages between job posters and workers, and direct messages sent to you by GoDayWork administrators
  • Ratings and reviews — feedback you leave for other users
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, IP address, and device/browser information
  • Deleted account records — if your account is removed by an administrator, your email address is retained in a blocklist to prevent re-registration

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the GoDayWork platform
  • To connect job posters with workers in their area
  • To send email notifications about jobs, applications, and account activity
  • To send verification codes when you create an account
  • To improve our service and fix bugs
  • To moderate content and enforce our Terms of Service

Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you personalized ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet. For more information, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

Payment Information

All payments on GoDayWork are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number or bank account details — all payment data is handled securely by Stripe. We collect and store only the following payment-related information:

  • Payment type — whether a job is posted as a flat rate or per-hour rate
  • Pay amount — the dollar amount stated in a job listing (maximum $200 per job)
  • Stripe Connect account ID — for workers who set up a payout account to receive payments
  • Payment status — whether a payment is authorized, held in escrow, or released

By using payment features, you also agree to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these cases:

  • Other users — your display name (first name and last initial only, e.g. "Jane S."), profile, and job posts are visible to other GoDayWork users. Your full legal name, phone number, and email are never shown publicly.
  • Service providers — we use Resend for email delivery, Railway for hosting, and Stripe for payment processing
  • Law enforcement — we will share full identity records (name, phone, photo, IP address, account activity) with law enforcement when legally required, including valid court orders, subpoenas, or situations involving immediate safety threats

Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Railway (railway.app). We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

Cookies

GoDayWork uses localStorage in your browser to keep you logged in and save your preferences. We also use cookies through Google AdSense for advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

Children's Privacy

GoDayWork is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of email notifications through your account settings
